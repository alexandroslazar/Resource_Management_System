# Collaboration Contract for Resource Management Project

## Team Overview

We are a team of four Computer Science students collaborating on a **Resource Management Project**. Our goal is to design and develop an efficient solution to streamline resource allocation, scheduling, and management.

## Tools and Platforms We Use

- **Google Docs**: Used for collaborative analysis and design documents, ensuring a single source of truth.
- **Discord**: Our primary communication platform for real-time discussions, meetings, and informal communication.
- **Notion**: Used for documentation, task tracking, and organizing project resources.
- **Jira**: Our tool for agile project management, used for planning sprints, assigning tasks, and tracking progress.
- **GitHub**: For version control and collaboration on code, keeping all project components in one place.

## Working Methodology: Agile Process

We follow an **Agile** approach to manage the project. Our work is organized into **sprints**, with regular **feedback** sessions and continuous improvements.

### Weekly Meetings

- **Purpose**: Discuss current progress, plan for the upcoming week, and address blockers.
- **Frequency**: Once a week (preferably at a consistent time, e.g., every Tuesday afternoon).
- **Format**: Each team member shares their progress, highlights challenges, and sets goals for the next sprint.
- **Attendance**: All team members are expected to attend the weekly meeting.

### Sprint Planning

- **Duration**: Each sprint will last for **1 week**, depending on task complexity.
- **Tasks**: At the beginning of each sprint, we will break down tasks in **Jira**, assign responsibilities, and set milestones.
- **Prioritization**: Tasks will be prioritized based on importance, dependencies, and deadlines.
- **Feedback**: After completing each sprint, we will review our progress, identify improvements, and adjust priorities for the next sprint.

### Communication

- **Discord**: For day-to-day communication, quick updates, and troubleshooting. Dedicated channels will be used to organize discussions by topics.
- **Notion**: For formal documentation, project tracking, and sharing resources. The Notion board will be updated with relevant information after each meeting.
- **Google Docs**: For collaborative writing, brainstorming, and storing design and analysis work.
- **GitHub**: For version-controlled code, ensuring all team members have access to the latest project components and changes.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### General Responsibilities

Each team member is responsible for:

- Actively contributing to project discussions and planning.
- Completing assigned tasks within the sprint deadlines.
- Participating in sprint reviews and retrospectives.
- Collaborating with team members to solve problems and resolve blockers.

### Individual Roles

While we collaborate as a team, we have individual areas of focus:

1. **Project Manager**: Oversees the project timeline, manages Jira tasks, and ensures the team stays on track.
2. **Software Architect**: Focuses on system architecture, database management, and implementing core logic.
3. **Tech Research Lead**: Researches technologies, develops the user interface, and ensures smooth user interactions.
4. **Design Lead**: Responsible for the user experience, writing test cases, running tests, and ensuring the quality of features.

## Code of Conduct

- **Respect**: We treat each other with respect, valuing everyone's input and contributions.
- **Transparency**: We maintain transparency regarding our progress, challenges, and any issues we face.
- **Collaboration**: We prioritize teamwork and communication, ensuring alignment with the project’s goals.
- **Constructive Feedback**: We offer and receive feedback in a constructive manner, focusing on personal and project improvement.

## Conflict Resolution

In case of disagreements or conflicts:

1. **Open Discussion**: Team members are encouraged to address concerns openly and respectfully.
2. **Resolution Process**: If a solution cannot be reached, the **Project Manager** will mediate to find a resolution or escalate the issue as needed.
3. **Focus on the Goal**: All conflicts should be resolved with the project’s success and team harmony in mind.

## Deadlines and Expectations

- **Task Completion**: Tasks must be completed by the end of each sprint. If a task cannot be completed, the team member must notify the group early and suggest potential solutions.
- **Quality Standards**: All code, documentation, and deliverables must meet agreed-upon quality standards.
- **Availability**: Team members should maintain clear communication regarding their availability, especially during critical milestones or deadlines.

## Feedback and Improvements

At the end of each sprint, we will hold a retrospective meeting to:

- **Celebrate Successes**: Acknowledge what went well and celebrate achievements.
- **Identify Challenges**: Discuss any blockers, inefficiencies, or issues encountered during the sprint.
- **Plan for Improvement**: Define action items to enhance our processes and workflows for the next sprint.

---

## Conclusion

This collaboration contract outlines how we will work together to ensure the success of the **Resource Management Project**. By following these guidelines, we aim to create a supportive, efficient, and productive working environment for all team members.

We commit to adhering to this contract and working towards our shared goals with dedication, respect, and professionalism.
